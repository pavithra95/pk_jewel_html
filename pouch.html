Potli Bags

Potli bags are widely used in weddings for return gifts, bridal accessories, and guest giveaways during functions like Mehendi, Haldi, and Sangeet ceremonies. Jewellery shops use them for premium packaging of gold, silver, diamond, and antique jewellery items, while corporate companies prefer customized potli bags for festive gifting, employee gifts, and client hampers during occasions like Diwali and New Year.

They are also popular as fashionable ethnic handbags that complement traditional outfits such as sarees, lehengas, and salwar suits. Gift and hamper businesses use potli bags for packing dry fruits, sweets, chocolates, cosmetics, and luxury gift items. Religious institutions and temples use them for prasad distribution, pooja kits, and sacred item storage, while hotels, resorts, boutiques, and event management companies use potli bags for welcome kits, designer packaging, and return gifts at various events.

Potli bags also serve as eco-friendly and reusable alternatives to plastic covers and disposable gift wraps.
