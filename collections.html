Our Collections

At Athira Enterprises, our collections of jewellery boxes and handcrafted pouches reflect elegance, purpose, and thoughtful design. Each piece is carefully created by skilled women artisans who bring dedication, creativity, and precision to every stitch and finish. By providing meaningful employment opportunities, we proudly support women empowerment and help strengthen local communities.

Our jewellery boxes are crafted to protect and present treasures beautifully, while our premium net drawstring pouches add charm and sophistication to gifting and packaging. We use quality materials, maintain strict finishing standards, and offer customization options to meet the needs of retailers, wedding planners, and corporate clients. Through every product, we combine craftsmanship with social responsibility—delivering not just packaging, but empowerment, trust, and lasting value.
