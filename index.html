About us

Welcome to Athira Enterprises., where craftsmanship meets elegance. We specialize in designing and manufacturing high-quality jewelry boxes and drawstring pouches that complement and enhance the beauty of precious jewelry pieces. With a commitment to exceptional craftsmanship and customer satisfaction, we offer a wide range of customizable solutions for retailers, brands, and individual customers. Established in the year 1997 at Nochully, Palakkad, Kerala We “Athira Enterprises” are the foremost Manufacturer of Drawstring Pouch and Jewelry boxes.
